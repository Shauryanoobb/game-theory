## TRAI — Telecom Regulatory Authority of India
Primary source. Quarterly "Performance Indicator Reports" (PIR).
- URL: https://www.trai.gov.in → Reports → Performance Indicator Reports
- Use: ARPU (wireless, all India, prepaid + postpaid), subscriber base, MoU (minutes of use), data volume per subscriber
- Relevant vintages: 2013 Q1 through 2024 Q4 (quarterly)
- Specific tables typically titled "Monthly ARPU by Service Provider"

## COAI — Cellular Operators Association of India
- URL: https://www.coai.com
- Use: industry aggregate reports, spectrum holdings, market-share breakdowns

## Company annual reports
- **Bharti Airtel:** https://www.airtel.in/about-bharti/equity/results  → India wireless ARPU, EBITDA margin
- **Reliance Jio (via RIL):** https://www.ril.com/InvestorRelations/FinancialReporting.aspx → Jio ARPU (reported from 2017 onwards)
- **Vodafone Idea:** https://www.myvi.in/about-us/investors → ARPU, subscriber churn, net debt

## CCI — Competition Commission of India
- Case No. 03/2017 (Bharti Airtel vs Reliance Jio) — predatory pricing complaint, dismissed
- URL pattern: https://www.cci.gov.in → Orders
- Use: for policy/legal section
